Dance Classes Program

Dance ClassesProgram

Dance classes are designed to promote physical fitness, coordination, and emotional expression in children. Through engaging styles such as Bollywood, Zumba, and Indian classical dance, children develop rhythm, balance, and flexibility. These sessions also help improve confidence, social interaction, and creativity while providing a fun and energetic environment. Dance is especially beneficial for enhancing motor skills and reducing stress in children.
